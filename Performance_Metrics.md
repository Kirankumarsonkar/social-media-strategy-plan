Performance Metrics

Overview

Performance metrics help measure the success of social media marketing campaigns and identify areas for improvement.

---

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

Reach Metrics

- Total Reach
- Impressions
- Profile Visits

Engagement Metrics

- Likes
- Comments
- Shares
- Saves
- Engagement Rate

Growth Metrics

- New Followers
- Follower Growth Rate
- Audience Retention

Traffic Metrics

- Website Clicks
- Landing Page Visits
- Referral Traffic

Conversion Metrics

- Leads Generated
- Form Submissions
- Sales Conversions
- Conversion Rate

---

Monthly KPI Targets

Metric| Monthly Target
Reach| 50,000+
Engagement Rate| 5%+
New Followers| 500+
Website Clicks| 1,000+
Leads Generated| 100+

---

Reporting Schedule

Weekly Reports

- Engagement Performance
- Audience Growth
- Top Performing Posts

Monthly Reports

- KPI Review
- Campaign Performance
- ROI Analysis

Quarterly Reports

- Strategy Review
- Competitor Benchmarking
- Goal Assessment

---

Success Measurement

Success will be evaluated based on:

- Increased audience engagement.
- Improved brand visibility.
- Higher website traffic.
- Better lead generation.
- Positive return on investment (ROI).

---

Conclusion

Tracking performance metrics enables continuous improvement, better decision-making, and more effective social media marketing campaigns.
