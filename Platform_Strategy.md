Platform Strategy

Overview

Each social media platform serves a different audience and purpose. A platform-specific strategy helps maximize reach, engagement, and conversions.

---

Facebook Strategy

Objectives

- Increase brand awareness
- Generate leads
- Build customer relationships

Content Types

- Educational posts
- Promotional offers
- Customer testimonials
- Live sessions

Posting Frequency

- 1–2 posts per day

---

Instagram Strategy

Objectives

- Increase engagement
- Build visual brand identity
- Reach younger audiences

Content Types

- Reels
- Stories
- Carousel posts
- Behind-the-scenes content

Posting Frequency

- 1 post daily
- Multiple stories per day

---

LinkedIn Strategy

Objectives

- Professional networking
- Industry authority building
- B2B lead generation

Content Types

- Industry insights
- Case studies
- Professional updates
- Articles

Posting Frequency

- 3–4 posts per week

---

YouTube Strategy

Objectives

- Long-form content marketing
- Audience education
- Brand credibility

Content Types

- Tutorials
- Product demonstrations
- Customer success stories
- Educational videos

Posting Frequency

- 1 video per week

---

X (Twitter) Strategy

Objectives

- Real-time engagement
- Industry discussions
- Brand visibility

Content Types

- Short updates
- News sharing
- Polls
- Community engagement

Posting Frequency

- 2–3 posts daily

---

Conclusion

A customized strategy for each platform ensures better audience engagement and improved marketing performance.
